Who are we ?

Honsen is created by a small french company called Motion-Twin. Based in Bordeaux, we're creating web games since 2001. Some of our web sites (in french) can be visited at the following URLs :

  • Motion-Twin (http://www.motion-twin.com)
  • Frutiparc (http://www.frutiparc.com)
  • KadoKado (http://www.kadokado.com)

Motion-Twin is a young and bright team of graphics designers and skilled programmers that are enjoying making games for a living. In order to push further the possibilities of web-based games, we have created Honsen and we hope it will be adopted by the community as a de-facto standard for creating 3D web contents.
