Want to give Honsen a try ? Check out the Samples section to see what can kind of things can be done using Honsen. Community

The Honsen Community is welcoming any good-willing people that want to provide feedback, ask questions, or simply discuss and ear about Honsen technology and next improvements.

You can join the Honsen mailing list by sending an email to list-request@honsen.org with "subscribe" in the subject. Once joined, you can send emails to list@honsen.org to ask questions.
